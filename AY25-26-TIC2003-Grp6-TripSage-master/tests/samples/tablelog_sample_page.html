But don't be fooled—our flavors and ingredients are top-notch! We use the Tsukamoto method for ultimate blood removal, sourcing fish directly from the producers. With careful preparation, we serve both fresh fish and flavorful aged fish.

Food ☆☆☆ Tsunamoto-style Ultimate Blood Removal Technique ★★★

At our shop, we use the Tsukamoto-style ultimate blood removal technique for all fresh fish. By performing this ultimate blood removal on fresh fish, we can reduce the fishy smell and enhance the flavor of the fish by storing it whole in water at low temperatures! We adopted the labor-intensive Tsukamoto-style ultimate blood removal because we truly want our customers to enjoy authentic flavors. When you visit, why not try our aged fish? It’s something that everyone becomes addicted to!

Food "Setting Prices with the Awareness of Red Ink!! We Will Serve Freshness Right Before Your Eyes."

We use carefully selected fish that has been aged to enhance its umami flavor. We serve it as two pieces, changing the seasoning for each piece to allow you to enjoy the flavors even more. Please experience the delicate differences in taste! Additionally, we use domestically sourced rice for our special white vinegar sushi rice, which has a natural sweetness that further enhances the flavor of the fish.

Drink"Sake Pairs Perfectly with Sushi: Rare Finds Not on the Menu!"

When it comes to enjoying seasonal sushi, you can't go without a companion, and that is definitely "sake"! At our restaurant, we offer a wide range of sake, from the classic and reasonably priced 【Shouchikubai】 to the popular 【Dassai】. Additionally, we have rare sake that is not on the grand menu, carefully selected by our chef every week! Enjoy your meal while savoring delicious sushi and fine sake!

Space"Say Cheese! The walls of the store are lined with memory Polaroids."

"Nigirite" is a very casual sushi restaurant where the owner always talks joyfully while making sushi, and customers who meet for the first time can chat with each other. Perhaps because of this atmosphere, we started taking memory photos with our customers, and before we knew it, the wall was filled with memories. The Nigirite family, who wants to continue enjoying sushi in a friendly manner, is always looking for new members! (In fact, this restaurant has experience appearing on "Monday Night Late Night"...)
